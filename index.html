MY FRIST DAY AT SCHOOL

My first day at scool was very interesting. I woke up early in the morning. My mother prepared my tiffin. I put on my uniform and had my breakfast. I went to the new school on foot with my father. After reaching there, I was very excited. My father introduced me with the hedmaster. He took me into my classroom and introduced me with my teacher and classmates. I talked with them. They were very friend and helpful. One of them made me his friend. In the tiffin time I shared my tiffin with my friend. A new school is a new experience and I really enjoy the day.It was an interesting.

TRAFFIC JAM

Traffic jam is a long ling of vehicles on a road. During traffic jam the vehicles cannot move forward or can move forward very slowly. Nowadays it is a common affair in our towns and cities especially in Dhaka city. The increasing number of vehicles mainly causes traffic jam. Narrow road, inadequate number of traffic police, zigzag roads, unlimited rickshaws, ignorance of the people about traffic rule etc are some of the causes of traffic jam. Especially it kills our valuable time. It brings sufferings for the serious patient. We should make our roads wide. The traffic police should be sincere. Above all, the passers-by and the drivers should follow traffic rules.

A MOON-LIT NIGHT

A moon-lit night is very charming. Tender is the night. It has pleasures. It pleases all man, women, children, birds and beasts. It feeds our eyes and fills our mind with immense joy and pleasure. We enjoy the night very much. It is like a day. We can move about from house to house. Boys play in the open fields. So a moon-lit night is a wonder of nature. It has special influence on poets and thinkers. Thy get the chance of writing poems and articles in a moon-lit night.

LOAD SHEDDING

Load-shedding mans irregular supply of electricity. It is a common affair in our country. It causes immense troubles to the people. It essentially brings serious loss to our economy. Low production is the main cause of load-shedding. Unplanned distribution, illegal connections, high demand are also the causes of it. We should set-up more electric plants. Distribution of electricity should be planned. Illegal connection should be disconnected.
